## Trading

1. Open the banking UI at any bank or ATM
2. Navigate to the **Market** section
3. View live prices and charts (7-day history)
4. **Buy** — Enter quantity, confirm purchase
5. **Sell** — Select from portfolio, confirm sale

### Trading Rules

- **Fee:** 0.5% per trade
- **Max portfolio value:** $500,000
- **Max order quantity:** 1,000 units per order
- Prices update every 5 minutes

## Term Deposits

| Duration | Interest |
|----------|----------|
| 7 days | 0.1% |
| 14 days | 0.3% |
| 30 days | 0.5% |
| 90 days | 1.0% |

### Rules

- Min deposit: $1,000
- Max deposit: $500,000
- Max 3 active deposits
- **Early withdrawal penalty:** 50% of principal
